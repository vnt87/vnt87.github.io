There are two key parts to every UX design project: conducting research to learn about the users you\u2019re designing for, and gathering feedback about their perspectives. UX design is all about putting the user first, and research helps designers understand those users. <\/p>\n\n\n\n

UX research<\/strong> focuses on understanding user behaviors, needs, and motivations through observation and feedback. Your product design should be built upon research and facts, not assumptions. UX research aligns what you, as the designer, think<\/em> the user needs with what the user actually<\/em> needs. <\/p>\n\n\n\n
