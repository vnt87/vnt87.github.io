I’ve burned through the last of my unemployment check<\/div>\n
My and my wife had burned through half of our savings trying to conceive a child (and bad coin investments).<\/div>\n
So far all had been for naught, and we might need another 100 mil for IVF.<\/div>\n
My dad still has kidney stones, might need surgery.<\/div>\n
