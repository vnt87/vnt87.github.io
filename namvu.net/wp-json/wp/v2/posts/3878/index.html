I recall the early days when pretty much anything and everything was new and exciting to us. Nowadays they merely seem like annoyances that you\u2019d do your best to avoid and carry on with your ordinary, uninteresting life.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

We need a change<\/p>\n\n\n\n
